# Projects

## Welgo
US property management — short-term rentals across Arizona and Colorado. $24K/month in rent obligations, distributed team across three time zones, seven bank accounts. I'm closing it deliberately. Not a failure story. A completion story.

## KamaRooms
108-room hotel turnaround in Tatarstan, Russia. Family business I stepped into as acting CTO — 9.4 on Booking.com, 15 disconnected systems I'm stitching together with spreadsheets and stubbornness. The real project turned out to be the guilt I feel when I'm not involved. That's identity work, not operations.

## Life OS
I replaced my executive assistant with an AI system built on Claude — email triage, morning briefings, weekly reviews, persistent memory across every project I run. Not a toy demo. The actual operating layer for my life. The bottleneck was never the tool — it was knowing myself well enough to tell the tool what matters.

## The Ed Letter
Writing about integration — the gap between knowing something and being able to use it. Published on LinkedIn. I share what I'm building while I'm building it, not after. The name for this is "Show My Work" and the biggest bottleneck is the publish button, not the ideas.
